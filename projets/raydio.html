Raydio

This website prototype finds a random community radio in the world and plays it for 30 seconds, then switch to another one.
It was made for a school research on how we could use community radios to empathize with worldwide local issues.
It is supposed to give a different look on information and focuses on randomness over algorithmic control.
It is to be outside the regular media and social media.
