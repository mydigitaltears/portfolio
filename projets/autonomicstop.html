Autonomic Stop

This audio-visual experience follows the narrative of sleep cycles over a regular night. Every hour was translated into two minutes.
Following the timeline provided by a regular sleeping app, the experience will intensify or calm up depending on the deepness level of the sleeping.
The audio and visuals are generated in real-time based on the sleep data and the heart rate of the user.
