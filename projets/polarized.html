Polarized

Polarized is a virtual being responsive to the voice and movents of the person in front of it.
It uses sentiment analysis to determine if the person said something positive or negative,
then it will respond with a random political tweet. Using the same sentiment analysis algorithm,
we organise different tweets into levels of polarization.
The voice and the movements of the being can change with the level of polarization of the tweets.
